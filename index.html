📖 Learn About SGPA & CGPA

Everything you need to know about SGPA, CGPA and Grade Points.

💡 Quick Facts

  • ✅ Maximum SGPA = 10.00
  • ✅ Minimum SGPA = 0.00
  • ✅ Higher credit subjects have more impact.
  • ✅ F Grade = 0 Grade Points.
  • ✅ SGPA is calculated semester-wise.
  • ✅ CGPA is calculated across all semesters.

📘 What is SGPA?

SGPA (Semester Grade Point Average) represents your academic performance in a single semester. It is calculated using the credits and grade points obtained in each subject.

🎓 What is CGPA?

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) is the average of all semester SGPAs throughout your degree and reflects your overall academic performance.

🧮 SGPA Formula

SGPA = Σ(Credit × Grade Point)

🧮 CGPA Formula

CGPA = Σ(SGPA × Semester Credits)

💡 Tips to Improve GPA

  • 📌 Focus more on high-credit subjects.
  • 📌 Complete assignments on time.
  • 📌 Track your SGPA every semester.
  • 📌 Never ignore low-credit subjects.
  • 📌 Maintain consistent performance.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the highest SGPA?

10.00

Can SGPA be more than 10?

No.

Does an F grade affect SGPA?

Yes. F grade carries 0 grade points.

Does every subject have equal weight?

No. Higher credit subjects contribute more.

Is SGPA the same as percentage?

No. Every university has its own conversion formula.
